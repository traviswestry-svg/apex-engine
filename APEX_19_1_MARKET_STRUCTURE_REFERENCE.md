# APEX 19.1 Market Structure Reference

## Outputs
- Multi-timeframe profiles: POC, VAH, VAL, HVN, LVN, width, price location, and confluence.
- Migration: rising, falling, flat, expanding, contracting, or mixed value.
- Opening type: Open Drive, Open Test Drive, Open Rejection Reverse, Open Auction in Range, or Open Auction in Range Expansion.
- Auction defects: poor high, poor low, single prints, buying tail, selling tail, and auction completion.
- Acceptance: acceptance above/below value, testing value boundaries, POC acceptance, or value rotation.
- Day type: trend-favored, balance-favored, or mixed probability.
- Levels: institutional supports, resistances, fast-travel LVNs, magnets, and projected upside/downside targets.

All outputs are advisory and deterministic over already-available APEX state.
